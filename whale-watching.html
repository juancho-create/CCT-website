Seasonal Adventure (July – Oct)

Whale Watching

A journey to Colombia's Pacific coast to witness the annual humpback whale migration in the warm waters of Bahía Málaga.

The Pacific Soul

Every year, hundreds of humpback whales travel over 8,000 km from Antarctica to the protected bays of Bahía Málaga to mate, give birth, and raise their calves. This is one of the most powerful natural spectacles on Earth. Join us for a full-day expedition from Cali to the rugged Pacific coast, where the jungle meets the sea.

The Expedition

01

Descent to the Pacific

Early morning pickup and a 3-hour drive from the Andes down to the port city of Buenaventura.

02

Bay Crossing

A fast speedboat transfer into the heart of Uramba Bahía Málaga National Park, surrounded by lush mangroves.

03

The Encounter

Board a specialized whale-watching boat with a certified local captain to witness breaching, tail-slapping, and nurturing behaviors.

04

Pacific Relaxation

Enjoy free time on a remote Pacific beach to swim, relax, and soak in the raw energy of the coast before returning to Cali.
