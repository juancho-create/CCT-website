Cali City Tour

From the towering peaks of Cristo Rey to the historic bohemian streets of San Antonio. Experience the heart and soul of Cali.

Cali From Above & Within

Discover Почему (Why) residents love this vibrant city. This half-day tour offers a perfect orientation, combining panoramic mountain views with the rich history of Cali's downtown and historic neighborhoods. You'll visit iconic monuments, learn about our diverse culture, and get a true feel for the city's rhythm.

Key Stops Included: Cristo Rey, Sebastián de Belalcázar, Trompetas de Niche, San Antonio Neighborhood, El Gato de Tejada, and Loma de la Cruz craft market.

The Route

01

Cristo Rey Ascent

Begin with a drive to the 26-meter Christ statue. enjoy 360-degree views of the entire Cauca Valley and the bustling city below.

02

Foundations & Heritage

Visit the Sebastián de Belalcázar statue to learn about Cali's founding and its complex colonial and indigenous history.

03

Bohemian San Antonio

Explore the colonial-style streets of Cali's oldest neighborhood. visit the iconic church and discover local artisan workshops.

04

Art & Artisans

Stroll past "The Cat" sculptures along the river and conclude at the Loma de la Cruz market—the best spot for authentic local handicrafts.
