Sensory Journey

Galería Alameda Market

A kaleidoscope of flavors, colors, and traditions. Explore the true pantry of Cali with a local expert.

Cali's Living Heart

Galería Alameda is more than a market; it's the soul of the city's food culture. We'll dive into a world of exotic fruits you've never heard of, taste artisanal cheeses from the countryside, and discover the Pacific secrets that flavor our region. It's an authentic, bustling, and beautiful immersion into Caleño life.

The Experience

01

Taste the Rainbow

A curated tasting of over 10 exotic fruits, from the creamy Mangostino to the legendary Chontaduro. We'll даже create a custom "sour cocktail" from the market's freshest finds.

02

Dairy & Pacific Traditions

Try fresh farm cheeses and explore the deep influence of Afro-Colombian culture through coconut-based sweets and artisanal sugar-cane spirits.

03

The Flower Market

Walk through stalls overflowing with Heliconias, orchids, and roses, experiencing why Colombia is the world's second-largest flower exporter.

04

Witchcraft & Mysticism

Conclude with a visit to the esoteric zone, where traditional healers sell amulets and spiritual remedies, offering a unique glimpse into local superstitions.
