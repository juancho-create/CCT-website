The Experience

01

Taste the Rainbow

A curated tasting of over 10 exotic fruits, from the creamy Mangostino to the legendary Chontaduro. We'll even create a custom "sour cocktail" from the market's freshest finds.

02

Dairy & Pacific Traditions

Try fresh farm cheeses and explore the deep influence of Afro-Colombian culture through coconut-based sweets and artisanal sugar-cane spirits.

03

The Flower Market

Walk through stalls overflowing with Heliconias, orchids, and roses, experiencing why Colombia is the world's second-largest flower exporter.

04

Witchcraft & Mysticism

Conclude with a visit to the esoteric zone, where traditional healers sell amulets and spiritual remedies, offering a unique glimpse into local superstitions.
