Wildlife Sanctuary

Hummingbird Paradise

A cloud forest sanctuary where nature flies free. Experience dozens of wild hummingbird species in their natural habitat.

About this experience

High in the western Andes, hidden within the cloud forest, lies a place where the air hums with life. Finca Alejandría is an open-air sanctuary—no cages, just nature—where wild hummingbirds gather in incredible numbers. It's a peaceful, magical immersion into Colombia's world-leading biodiversity.

The Journey

01

Mountain Ascent

A scenic drive from Cali into the western Andes, transitioning from the city's warmth to the cool, fresh air of the cloud forest.

02

Goat Farm Visit

A charming stop to meet, pet, and feed baby goats—a delightful moment of rural Caleño life.

03

The Sanctuary

Spend time at the sanctuary observing dozens of hummingbirds at arm's length. Enjoy local coffee while watching colorful tanagers and mountain birds.

04

Sweet Return

A stop for traditional local desserts on the way back down the mountain, concluding your nature escape.
