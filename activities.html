The Cali Weekly Calendar

From salsa nights to hidden nature escapes, here's how to make the most of every day in the city.

Monday

Alameda Market Discovery

The best time for a focused market visit. Fewer crowds mean more time for fruit tastings and stories.

Explore Market Tour
Tuesday

Salsa Genesis

The perfect day to start your dance journey. Many schools open their doors for beginner-only sessions.

Ask for School Tips

Street Food Evenings

A mid-week culinary adventure. Discover the snacks and stories that fuel the city's spirit.

Join Food Tour

Live Rhythm Night

Cali starts to wake up for the weekend. Catch soul-stirring live performances in San Antonio and Granada.

The Salsa Weekend

Experience the legendary Friday night energy. This is when the city truly becomes the World Capital of Salsa.

Book Salsa Experience

Nature Escape

Head to the hills. Saturday is for waterfalls, bird santuaries, and the lush Farallones mountains.

Crafts & Sunset

Visit Loma de la Cruz for artisanal crafts and the best sunset views, accompanied by local drumming circles.
