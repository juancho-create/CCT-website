Farm to Bar

Cacao Experience

A hands-on journey from the cacao tree to your own custom chocolate bar. Discover the magic of Colombian cacao.

The Art of Chocolate

This is more than a farm visit; it's a deep dive into one of Colombia's most cherished traditions. You'll walk through lush cacao groves, learn the intricate process of fermentation and drying, and finally, craft your very own chocolate bar using traditional and modern techniques. It's a sweet, educational, and deeply rewarding experience for all ages.

The Journey

01

Into the Groves

Arrive at the farm and walk among the cacao trees. Learn how to identify ripe pods and see the harvesting process in action.

02

Fermentation & Drying

Discover the "behind-the-scenes" magic that develops the complex flavors of Colombian cacao through expert fermentation and sun-drying.

03

Crafting Your Bar

The highlight of the day! Roast, grind, and temper your own batch. Mix in your favorite flavors and pour into molds to create your custom masterpiece.

04

Tasting & Heritage

Conclude with a guided chocolate tasting, comparing different cacao percentages and learning about sustainable farming practices in the region.
