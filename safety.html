First, the Facts

While opinions vary, statistics offer a clearer comparison. Cali's evolution over the last decade shows a significant trend toward safety:

  • 63% Decrease in homicide rates since the peak of the cartel era (1994 vs 2023).
  • 68.3% Reduction in automobile theft from 2021 to 2022.
  • Lower rates of robberies and phone thefts compared to Bogotá and Medellín.
  • Statistically safer than several major US cities including New Orleans, Baltimore, and Detroit.
  • Cali is not in Colombia's top 5 for homicide, theft, or threat rates.

Areas & Navigation

Understanding the city's geography is key. The far east of the city and specific mountain neighborhoods are the primary areas to avoid. Fortunately, these are far from the main tourist circuits and cultural hubs.
