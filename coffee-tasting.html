Coffee Tasting

A guided journey through Colombia's specialty coffee world. Learn to identify notes, aromas, and profiles from a professional barista.

The Art of the Cup

You don't need to leave the city to experience the soul of Colombian coffee. This intimate, seated tasting in Cali is led by a professional barista who will guide you through the complexities of our most famous export. From understanding SCA quality scores to mastering different brewing methods, you'll gain the knowledge to appreciate coffee wherever your travels take you.

The Masterclass

01

Coffee Foundations

A warm welcome and exploration of the coffee plant, altitude's role, and how harvesting defines the potential of the bean.

02

Flavor Architecture

Learn to identify acidity, sweetness, and body. Discover how different processing methods—washed, honey, and natural—change everything.

03

The Brewing Ritual

Hands-on demonstration of V60 and Chemex methods. See (and taste) how temperature and technique unlock different flavor profiles.

04

Tasting & Local Pairing

Sample 2-3 distinct single-origin coffees, paired with a traditional Colombian pandebono snack. Conclude with a personalized Q&A.
