☕ Cali Coffee Tasting

Discover Colombian coffee through a guided tasting led by a professional barista — no farm, no long walks, just great coffee.

Quick Info

  • Duration: 1.5 – 2 hours (seated experience, no walking)
  • Price: 120,000 COP per person (~$30 USD)
  • Payment: Card or PayPal (COP, USD, EUR accepted)
  • Discount: 10% off paying pesos cash · 5% off USD/EUR cash
  • Location: Centro Comercial Centenario, Cali
  • Tastings: 2–3 different Colombian specialty coffees
  • Difficulty: Easy — fully seated, ideal for all travelers
  • Languages: English & Spanish

What's Included

  • 2–3 cups of Colombian specialty coffee (different profiles and brewing methods)
  • Choose your own coffee to brew
  • Brew your own cup step-by-step
  • Traditional pandebono snack included
  • Private bilingual guide throughout
  • Direct access to a professional Colombian barista for Q&A

What You Will Learn

  • What is coffee? — From plant to cup
  • What makes a great coffee: altitude, variety, harvesting, processing, roasting
  • Why Colombian coffee is world-famous
  • Caffeine vs. over-roasting — how roast levels affect flavor
  • Coffee flavor notes & profiles — acidity, sweetness, body, aftertaste
  • SCA Score (Specialty Coffee Association) — what quality grades really mean
  • How to spot a good coffee anywhere you travel
  • Coffee processing methods: washed, honey, natural
  • Brewing methods: V60, Chemex and how they change the final taste

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this a coffee farm tour? No. It's a tasting experience in Cali at a specialty café — ideal for travelers without time to visit the coffee region.
  • Do I need coffee knowledge? Not at all. Designed for beginners and coffee lovers alike.
  • How many coffees will we taste? 2–3 different Colombian specialty coffees with different brewing methods.
  • Is there any walking? No. Fully seated and relaxed.

Why Join This Tour?

  • Perfect if you don't have time to visit the coffee region
  • Relaxed seated experience
  • Taste multiple Colombian coffees in one session
  • Learn directly from a local barista
  • Guided in clear international English
