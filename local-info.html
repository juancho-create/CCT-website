Local Knowledge

Master the art of living like a local in Cali. From etiquette and weather to the unwritten rules of the city.

Kindness is the currency here. Master these cultural nuances to connect more deeply with the locals:

The Greeting Culture

A "Hola" or "Buenos días" goes a long way. We appreciate every effort to speak Spanish, no matter how small.

Transportation Etiquette

When using apps like Uber, riding in the front seat is appreciated. And a tip for American guests: our car doors are lighterno need to slam them!

The Indirect "No"

CaleÃ±os are rarely straightforward with a "no." If someone is being vague, it's often their way of being polite.

Weather & Style

Cali is "The Eternal Warm." There are no seasons, only altitude-controlled microclimates. Expect warm days and refreshing tropical rains that clear as quickly as they arrive.

Club Dress Code

While we love summer weather, we take nightlife style seriously. Tank tops and sandals are often sent back homedress to impress if you're hitting the salsa clubs.

"No Dar Papaya"

The golden rule of Colombia: don't put yourself at risk. Keep tech and jewelry low-profile, take Ubers after dark, and trust your local guide's intuition on where to explore.
