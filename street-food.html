Street Food & Stories

Taste the real Cali. A walking culinary journey through the neighborhoods and flavors that define our soul.

About this experience

This walking tour is a sensory exploration of Cali's street life. We'll wander through historic neighborhoods, meeting the vendors and artisans who have kept our traditions alive for generations. From crispy empanadas to ancestral Viche moonshine, every bite tells a story of community and resilience.
