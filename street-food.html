Culinary Adventure

Street Food & Stories

Taste the real Cali. A walking culinary journey through the neighborhoods and flavors that define our soul.

The Flavor of Cali

This walking tour is a sensory exploration of Cali's street life. We'll wander through historic neighborhoods, meeting the vendors and artisans who have kept our traditions alive for generations. From crispy empanadas to ancestral Viche moonshine, every bite tells a story of community and resilience.

The Experience

1

Salsa & Street Art

Walk the Boulevard del Río and discover the creative resilience of Barrio Obrero's visual culture at La Linterna poster workshop.

2

The Heritage Quarter

Explore San Antonio's cobblestone streets, visiting family-owned bakeries and community projects that protect our architecture.

3

Ancestral Tastes

Experience a guided Viche tasting—the ancestral spirit of the Pacific—and learn about its cultural significance.

4

Fritanga Feast

Finish with a selection of Cali's finest fried snacks (aborrajados, empanadas) and a refreshing local fruit drink.
