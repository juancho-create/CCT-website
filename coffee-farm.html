Coffee Country

Coffee Heritage

A full-day immersion in Sevilla, the "Balcony of the Valley." Discover the journey from seed to cup in the heart of the Andes.

The Spirit of the Bean

Sevilla is more than a coffee town; it's a living monument to Colombian heritage. On this full-day tour, we'll journey deep into the mountains to a working plantation. You won't just watch; you'll participate in every step—from picking the ripe cherries to the final, aromatic brew. It's an authentic encounter with the culture that defined our nation.

The Journey

01

Mountain Drive

A scenic 3-hour journey through the rolling hills of the Valle del Cauca, pausing for a traditional roadside breakfast with the locals.

02

Farm Immersion

Arrive at our partner farm in Sevilla. Walk the plantation, learn about sustainable cultivation, and hands-on pick your own coffee cherries.

03

The Processing Art

Follow the bean through harvesting, depulping, drying, and roasting. Understand the craftsmanship required to produce world-class specialty coffee.

04

Barista Session & Views

Conclude with a professional tasting led by a local barista. Finally, enjoy panoramic views from the "Balcony of the Valley" before our return.
