About My Business & Myself

Hi! My name is Juan Camilo and I'm born and raised in Cali, Colombia. Since 2022 I've been dedicating most of my time to tourism.

Tourism has given me opportunity to meet people from all over world and showcase my culture and city to them. I love eating, I love animals (I melt on the sight of a dog!), and I love being in nature — that's why I have a waterfalls tour and a hummingbird tour.

I love showing my city to visitors. Feel free to contact me, even if you don't do a tour with me, I'll be more than happy to assist you. My biggest asset is flexibility, English, and personalization.

I have a nickname for it: Cali COOLtural Tours.
