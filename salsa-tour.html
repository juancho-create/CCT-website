Cultural Experience

Cali Salsa Tour

Step into the World Capital of Salsa. A journey through rhythm, history, and the soul of the city.

Experience the Rhythm

This isn't just a tour; it's an immersion. We'll trace the roots of salsa in Cali, from the legendary Grupo Niche to the local record shops where vinyl is still king. Finally, you'll put your knowledge to the test with a session at one of the city's most iconic dance spots.

The Journey

01

Jairo Varela Plaza

Meet at the iconic "Salsa Trumpets" monument to begin our exploration of the city's musical heart.

02

Salsa Museum Immersion

A guided visit to either the Jairo Varela Museum or the Barrio Obrero Salsa Museum, meeting the local legends who keep the history alive.

03

Vinyl Culture

Visit a classic record shop to discover rare LPs and hear the sounds that defined an era.

04

Dance Class

A group salsa class at Topa Tolondra (or similar), perfect for all levels to learn the unique Caleño style.
