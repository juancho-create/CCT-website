⛰️ Pance River Waterfall Tour — Chorrera del Indio

Crystal-clear pools and waterfalls in the foothills of Farallones de Cali National Park.

Quick Info

  • Duration: 4 hours (morning or afternoon departure)
  • Price (1st person): 306,900 COP (~$76 USD)
  • Each additional: 104,900 COP (~$26 USD)
  • Children: Up to 12 years — 50% off
  • Payment: Cash (COP/USD), Card, PayPal
  • Discount: 10% off paying pesos cash · 5% off USD/EUR cash
  • Difficulty: Easy — flat trails with optional steep 5-min hike to 2nd waterfall
  • Languages: English & Spanish

What's Included

Round-trip transportation from Cali
Bilingual guide
Entrance fee
Local snacks
Medical insurance coverage

Optional Add-ons

Professional Drone Video 280,000 COP (~$68 USD)
Traditional Breakfast 80,000 COP (~$20 USD, adds 1 hour)

Itinerary

  1. Pickup in Cali — 25–60 min drive (ends with unpaved road)
  2. Public River park (1 hour) — walk through lush nature as a caleño would
  3. Waterfall time (1 hour) — swim in natural pools, explore Chorrera del Indio, optional steep 5-min hike to the hidden 2nd waterfall
  4. Return to Cali — 1-hour drive

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes for swimming
  • Towel
  • Non-slip water shoes or hiking sandals
  • Change of clothes
  • Hat or cap
  • Eco-friendly insect repellent
  • Waterproof phone case (optional)

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is it from Cali?

25 to 60 minutes depending on your pickup location and traffic.

Is it difficult to reach the waterfalls?

The first waterfall is a short walk from parking. The 2nd requires a 5-minute steep hike (optional).

Can kids join?

Yes — fully family-friendly. Children love the natural pools.

What if it rains?

Tours operate rain or shine. In heavy storms, waterfall access may be restricted for safety.
