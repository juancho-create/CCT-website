Nature Escape

Pance River Waterfalls

Crystal-clear pools and hidden cascades. Refresh your soul in the foothills of the Farallones de Cali National Park.

About this experience

Escape the city's heat and dive into the refreshing Pance River. This tour takes you to the Chorrera del Indio, a stunning waterfall surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. It's the perfect balance of light adventure and deep relaxation in one of Cali's most beloved natural spots.

The Journey

01

Cali to Pance

A short, scenic drive from the city towards the southern foothills of the Farallones mountains.

02

River Walk

A gentle walk through the public river park, experiencing Pance as the locals dosurrounded by birds and the sound of flowing water.

03

Chorrera del Indio

Swim in natural crystal-clear pools and feel the energy of the main waterfall. For the adventurous, a short hike reveals a hidden second cascade.

04

Local Refresher

Enjoy traditional local snacks by the river before our comfortable return to the city.
